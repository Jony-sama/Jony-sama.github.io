The way of our idea and SDX

In the last years we get bored about the default drinks in Switzerland. So we decided to make it more excited and started to mix drinks and special ingredients together. The aim was to create special drinks with an exotic flavour which you can't find in swiss shops. It was clear for us that we have to seek also abroad to find exotic flavours and drinks. After we mixed a lot, chatted with suppliers in different countries and got motivated feedback, we started to sell on spot mixtures at small events. Soon we got requets by clubs for our mixed drinks and even for imported exotic drinks. The demand increased in the last year rapidly and we decided to spread out our drinks with this webshop.
